About Us

Elite IAS Academy: Your Pathway to Civil Services Excellence

Elite IAS Academy is a renowned coaching institute located in Delhi, with a singular focus – preparing aspirants for the Union Public Service Commission
(UPSC) exams. Recognized as one of the best IAS coaching institutes in Delhi, our core mission is twofold: to equip students with the comprehensive knowledge
they require, and to instill in them the confidence and strategic thinking crucial for succeeding in the Civil Services Examination.

Founding Story

Founded under the visionary leadership of Bibhash Sharma and Ravi Chamoli, Elite IAS Academy set out to address the pressing need for quality and
personalized UPSC coaching in Delhi. The academy came into being as a response to a glaring gap in specialized UPSC training programs, and the founders’
recognition that aspirants were in dire need of an educational environment that was not just informative but also transformative.
